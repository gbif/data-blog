# What is it? 

A place for GBIF staff and guest bloggers to contribute: 

* Statistics
* Graphs
* Tutorials 
* Ideas 
* Opinions 

# Who can contribute? 

If you would like to contribute you can contact jwaller@gbif.org. **Guest posts are very welcome.**
